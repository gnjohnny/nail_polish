Services

(1)Gel Manicure

A gel manicure involves applying a special type of gel-based nail polish. This polish is cured (hardened) under a UV or LED light after each coat, making the polish set instantly.

Gel manicures are known for their durability and can last up to 2-3 weeks without chipping or peeling. They are ideal for those looking for long-lasting, glossy nails.

The removal process for gel polish requires soaking the nails in acetone or wrapping them in acetone-soaked cotton and aluminum foil, as it cannot be easily removed with regular nail polish remover.

(2) Nail Art

Nail Art is a form of creative expression where intricate designs, patterns, and embellishments are applied to nails. It is become a major trend in the beauty and fashion industries, allowing people to showcase their personality, style, and creativity through their nails. Nail art can range from simple designs to complex and detailed artwork..
